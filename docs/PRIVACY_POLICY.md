# Privacy Policy — Second Chat

**Last updated:** May 2026

Second Chat (“we”, “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains in simple terms what information we may collect when you use our app and how we use it.

**Contact:** secondchatcontact@gmail.com

---

**What we collect**

- Basic account and sign-in details when you log in (for example with Google).
- Information needed to link your streaming or social accounts (such as Twitch, Kick, or YouTube) when you choose to connect them.
- Device-related basics needed to run the app (for example device type, app version, language).
- If you allow notifications, a device token so we can send you push messages.
- Content you create or send inside the app (for example chat, invites, or similar features), as needed for those features to work.

We may use trusted third-party services (for example Google and cloud tools) to host data, send notifications, and keep the app running. Those providers handle some information under their own terms and privacy rules.

---

**How we use it**

We use this information only to run Second Chat, improve the experience, keep accounts secure, and follow the law. We do not sell your personal information.

---

**Storage and security**

Some data stays on your phone (for example login session or settings). Other data is stored on our systems or our providers’ systems. We use reasonable security steps, but no online service can be 100% risk-free.

---

**Children**

Second Chat is not meant for children under 13. If you think a child has given us personal data, email us and we will help remove it.

---

**Your choices**

You can turn off push notifications in your phone settings. You can disconnect linked accounts in the app where that option exists. For other questions or to ask about your data, email **secondchatcontact@gmail.com**.

---

**Changes**

We may update this policy sometimes. When we do, we will change the date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy where the law allows.

---
